What is a liverpool fan?
</font>
</br>
<font size=14 color=white?
As a reference to the supporters who formerly stood and currently sit on the Kop at Anfield, Liverpool supporters frequently refer to themselves as Kopites. A group of supporters founded A.F.C. Liverpool in 2008 as a splinter club to provide Premier League football matches to supporters who had been priced out of the sport.
